Challenge

The biggest challenge of this lab was figuring out how to place my grave (task x) image under my zombie (center-both) image. At first I tried to center the grave the same way as the zombie with just one div, but realized the initial div that resized the image needed another div around it to correctly orient it's position on the page rather than around the image.

Problems

During this lab we had problems with positioning the center-both image so that it was actually centered. Due to the image's transparent background, it's actual width was different than it's appearance so the "left" and "top" percentages needed a slight adjustment from 50%.

Results
