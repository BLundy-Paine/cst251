My name is Benjamin and I think baking is the best hobbie, because when you're finished you get a delicious treat. It is also a great opportunity to share something enjoyable with those you love!

Many of my memories growing up include sitting on my kitchen counter making cookies with my mom, who didn't cook that often. The Quaker oatmeal canister would come down and we'd make the classic "Vanishing Oatmeal Cookies", sub chocolate chips for the raisins. Those cookies would be enjoyed for days until my dad would inevitably eat them in the night.

Try making them yourself to warm your heart and soul.
